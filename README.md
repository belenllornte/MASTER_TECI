## Overview

Welcome! This repository serves as a comprehensive portfolio of all my projects, coursework, and practical assignments completed during my master.

The TECI program is a specialized, joint-degree master's from two of Spain's leading universities, uniquely bridging the worlds of formal mathematics and applied engineering. It is co-directed by the Faculty of Mathematical Sciences (UCM) and the School of Telecommunications Engineering (UPM). The curriculum focuses on advanced techniques for data analysis, computational modeling, and information processing, taught from both a rigorous theoretical perspective and a practical, hands-on engineering one.

The primary goal of this repository is to:
* Document my learning journey and the practical application of complex theories.
* Organize all academic materials, code, and reports in a clean, accessible structure.
* Showcase my skills in advanced data science, optimization, and computational methods to peers, professors, and potential employers.
